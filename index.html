Juan Deng is a Lecturer with the School of Computer Engineering, Chengdu Technological University, Chengdu, China.

Her research interests lie in recommendation systems and machine learning for ecological protection. She teaches Recommendation Systems (48-hour course) for undergraduate students, covering classical collaborative filtering, deep learning-based ranking models, and hands-on system implementation.
